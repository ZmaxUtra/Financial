Types of Investments

Stocks

Company shares offering potential growth and dividends. Higher risk, higher potential return.

Bonds

Fixed-income securities with regular interest payments. Lower risk, stable returns.

Mutual Funds

Professionally managed investment pools. Diversified exposure to multiple assets.
